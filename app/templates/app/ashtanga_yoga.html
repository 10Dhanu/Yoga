Ashtanga Yoga

Ashtanga Yoga is a modern form of classical Indian yoga that focuses on synchronizing breath with a dynamic series of postures (asanas). It is physically demanding, structured, and follows a set sequence of poses, making it ideal for building strength, flexibility, and endurance.

Benefits :

  • Builds Core Strength & Flexibility
  • Increases Stamina & Endurance
  • Promotes Detoxification Through Sweat
  • Encourages Self-Discipline & Consistency
